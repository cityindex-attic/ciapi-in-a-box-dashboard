Concepts

Market

An instrument on which you can place a trade or order.  Includes equities such as Vodaphone Plc, indicies like the Dow Jones, currency pairs like USD/GBP. 

Price

The price at which you can buy or sell at for a particular market, at a particular point in time.  Market prices are based on the underlying exchange market price; but adjusted to include our comission.  In addition to publishing real-time prices for each of our markets, you can also get historic prices in a format suitable for plotting financial charts.

Order

An instruction to buy or sell a certain quantity of a particular market at a certain trigger point in the future.  The trigger point could be events such as the market price reaching a certain level.

Trade

An order which has been executed.  Placing a trade is the same as placing an order whoes trigger point is now.

News

Financial news stories.

Session

To access any data feed, you must authenticate each requests with a valid session key.  A session key is obtained from a logon call to the API, providing the client's UserName and Password.  The session key is valid until it expires, or a logout call is made.
